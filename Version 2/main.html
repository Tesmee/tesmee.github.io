Rules

1. Flip the cards to reveal hidden words underneath and understand their meanings.

2. Select exactly 3 cards that you think form a valid combination or share a common connection.

3. Once you’ve chosen your cards, press the submit button to check if your selection is correct.

4. If your set is correct, the cards will disappear from the board. If it’s incorrect, they will simply flip back to their original state.

5. Continue finding all possible valid combinations until you clear the entire board and complete the game.


Best played on larger screens- laptops and computers

The App Itself

Windows: If the app is unverified, Windows may show “Windows protected your PC.” Click “More info” → “Run anyway.” If needed, right-click → “Properties” → check “Unblock” → Apply.

Mac: macOS may block it with “unidentified developer.” Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → click “Open Anyway,” or right-click the app → “Open.”

It may take time to open because Python + PySide6 apps need to start the Python engine and load large Qt libraries before the window appears, unlike native apps that run directly.
